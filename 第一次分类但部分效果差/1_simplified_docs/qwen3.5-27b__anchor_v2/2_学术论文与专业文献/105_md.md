中国经济高速发展推动快餐业崛起，2012 年占百强餐饮额 46.4%，但微利时代竞争激烈，外送服务成竞争焦点。针对高校外卖乱象（浪费、安全、交通），基于江苏及云南大学实证调研显示：90 后是主力军，消费受口味、价格、时间、食堂拥挤度驱动。体验维度含餐品、包装、配送、卫生等七要素。优化路径需构建敏捷物流、强化过程控制与绿色包装。技术端借助大数据实现动态定价与分层运营，如"拼好饭"模式。未来趋势指向 O2O 融合、资源共享及物联网赋能，强调以用户为中心提升全链条服务质量。

Key Findings:
1. **Market Status**: Fast food holds 1/3 of catering output; delivery is the new growth engine amidst margin pressure.
2. **Pain Points**: Poor logistics, high food waste (especially on campuses), safety concerns, and environmental impact.
3. **Consumer Insights**: Post-70s/80s and Gen Z drive demand via mobile; key drivers include taste, price sensitivity, and convenience over canteen crowds.
4. **Optimization**: Requires automated dynamic pricing, IoT integration for smart lockers, sustainable packaging, and robust quality control systems to enhance customer retention.
