淤泥质土固化及路用性能试验研究
徐日庆1，4†等（1.浙江大学；2.杭州市城市基础设施建设管理中心；3.中国电建集团华东勘测设计研究院有限公司；4.浙江省城市地下空间开发工程技术研究中心）
摘要：针对台州淤泥质土，选取高炉矿渣、粉煤灰和电石渣为固化剂。混料试验得14d和28d龄期无侧限抗压强度回归方程。最佳配比ZX20（高炉矿渣∶粉煤灰∶电石渣=42.1∶11.5∶46.4），28d强度达1479.32kPa。对比二灰土，ZX20满足路基填筑要求，水稳性（K=0.86）及干湿循环性能更优。强度与龄期呈对数关系拟合良好（R²=0.978）。SEM与PCAS分析表明，随龄期生成丝状、针状及絮凝物，分割大孔隙并填充，提升强度。
关键词：工业废料；路基土；淤泥质土；无侧限抗压强度

Experimental Study on Solidification and Road Performance of Mucky Soil
XU Riqing1,4† et al. (1.Zhejiang Univ.; 2.Hangzhou Municipal Infra; 3.Power China Huadong Eng; 4.Zhejiang Urban Underground Dev)
Abstract: Blast furnace slag, fly ash, and calcium carbide slag cured Taizhou mucky soil. Regression equations for unconfined compressive strength at 14d and 28d were obtained. Optimal ratio ZX20 (slag:fly ash:carbide=42.1:11.5:46.4) yielded 1479.32 kPa at 28d. Compared to lime-lime soil, ZX20 met subgrade requirements with superior water stability (K=0.86) and dry-wet cycle performance. Strength-age relationship fitted well by logarithmic function (R²=0.978). SEM and PCAS showed filaments, needles, and floccules generated over time, dividing large pores into small ones and filling voids to increase strength.
Key words: industrial waste; subgrade soil; mucky soil; unconfined compressive strength
